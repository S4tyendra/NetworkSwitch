<p><i>Network Switch</i> provides control over 34 network modes including 2G, 3G, 4G, and 5G configurations. Works with both Root and Shizuku permissions.</p><p><b>Features</b></p><ul><li>34 network modes: Basic (2G/3G/4G/5G), Combined, Regional (CDMA, TD-SCDMA), Global</li><li>Configurable toggle between any two modes</li><li>Quick Settings tile with current/next mode display</li><li>Battery and speed optimization through mode selection</li><li>Modern Material Design 3 interface</li><li>Privacy-focused (no internet permissions)</li><li>Compatible with rooted (Root) and non-rooted (Shizuku) devices</li></ul><p><b>Use Cases</b></p><p>Optimize battery life, maximize speed, improve coverage in poor signal areas, manage data usage, and test network compatibility.</p>
